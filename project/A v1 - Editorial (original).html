01 — About

A writer, producer & director working at the intersection of culture, identity & storytelling.

With a background in digital media, a philosophy degree, and nearly a decade of producing stories that amplify underrepresented voices.

I lead and produce projects that foreground the stories of women and communities that rarely get to be seen on their own terms.

Whether producing long-form documentaries or multi-platform content across social, editorial, branded, and experiential formats, my work is driven by cultural shifts, community-led storytelling, and a clear point of view.

07 — Events & Curation

Cultural events, installations & programming.

A World of Womena

A multi-sensory exhibition for Dubai Design Week 2024 transforming you into a world of Womena through storytelling, design, expression and audio-visual mediums. Curated for Womena's 10-year anniversary as an impact-driven media platform.

The exhibition invites you to engage with the stories of artists Life of Loofah, Kilma and Solenne Tadros — powerful narratives celebrating women's self-expression & resilience.
