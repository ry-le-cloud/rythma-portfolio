Editorial Thinking

How I think about stories.

I'm interested in stories that sit at the intersection of culture, identity, politics, and emotional truth. My work is rooted in research-driven storytelling and editorial clarity — balancing strong creative direction with narratives that feel human, nuanced, and culturally grounded.

Whether developing a film, branded project, podcast, or editorial series, I approach storytelling as both a creative and structural process: shaping not just visuals, but perspective, tone, pacing, and emotional resonance.
