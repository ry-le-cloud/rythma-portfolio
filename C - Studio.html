↳ ABOUT

A writer, creative producer and director working at the intersection of culture, identity and storytelling across the MENA region and its diaspora.

A background in digital media, a philosophy degree, and nearly a decade of producing stories that amplify underrepresented voices.

I lead and produce projects that foreground the stories of women and communities that rarely get to be seen on their own terms — long-form documentary, multi-platform content across social, editorial, branded, and experiential formats.
